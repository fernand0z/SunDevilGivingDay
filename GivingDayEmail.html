Sun Devil Giving Day

Every year, thousands of loyal Sun Devils – alumni, parents, faculty, staff, students and fans – come together to accelerate the process of changing lives and the future as part of Arizona State University’s Sun Devil Giving Day, a 24-hour fundraising event.

As we await the fifth-annual day of giving, we encourage you to make a contribution. All the funds raised will enable our college to ensure access for all deserving students, foster creativity and innovation across disciplines and leverage our talents to promote social progress and economic growth.
